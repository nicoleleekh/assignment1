About Wild Wild Wet

Wild Wild Wet is one of Singapore’s largest water parks that promises a day of thrills and spills for the whole family. Recently expanded and sitting on 4 hectares of land, the water park is home to 16 water rides for all age groups including 7 latest additions that promise to bring excitement to a whole new level for everyone. From adrenaline-pumping rides like Kraken Racers - Singapore's first four-lane mat racer slide or Royal Flush - Asia's first hybrid ride featuring both the Behemoth BOWL40 and TornadoWAVE rides in one to relaxing and gentle ones like Shiok River, visitors are guaranteed a splashing fun-filled day!

Wild Wild Wet is located at 1 Pasir Ris Close, Singapore 519599.
